Hockey is a diverse sport

In terms of international diversity Hockey can be viewed as diverse. Hockey is a sport which can be picked up and played anywhere in the world.

Facts

In 2016 it was reported the amount of Canadian players, only in the NHL, had deminissed to 43%. Furthermore, it's estimated 35% of the sport is built of international players, more diverse than anyother sport in the United States or Canada.

Misconception

The common misconception throughout time has been Hockey is strictly Canadian because they make up all the Hockey players in the world, and if not Canadian then American.

Truth of the matter

As it has been shown Hockey, for example the NHL, is built up of a diverse group of people. Therefore, it's discouraging to anyone who wishes to play the sport because they would be seen as an outlier. No one would wish to participate in a sport if they didn't belong and would rediculed for it.
